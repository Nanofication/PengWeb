Our Founder’s Passion

Mr Li, the founder of our company, has a deep passion for rescuing and caring for stray animals. He firmly believes that the most despicable individuals are those who adopt pets out of fleeting affection, only to abandon them when their interest fades. Over the years, he has provided a home to countless stray animals, driven by his unwavering compassion.

During his frequent trips to purchase pet supplies, he connected with many like-minded individuals who shared his love for animals. These interactions not only strengthened his dedication but also helped him gain extensive knowledge about pet products. Over time, he became an expert in the field.

Inspired by his experiences and fueled by his desire to make a difference, he decided to establish his own pet company. His mission is to offer high-quality, reliable pet products at affordable prices for fellow pet lovers. By doing so, he not only supports the needs of pet owners but also generates the resources necessary to continue rescuing and caring for stray animals.

This venture allows him to combine his passion with purpose—making a living while making a lasting impact on the lives of countless animals in need.

Our Vision

At PengYu Global, we envision a world where every pet leads a happy, healthy, and enriched life, supported by our thoughtfully curated selection of products and our unwavering commitment to exceptional customer service.

  • Provide a seamless online shopping experience that delights pet owners and meets the unique needs of every pet.
  • Continuously innovate our product line to address emerging pet care trends and promote responsible pet ownership.
  • Foster a community of passionate pet lovers who share knowledge, experiences, and support for the well-being of all animals.
  • Lead the industry in sustainable and eco-friendly pet product solutions, minimizing our environmental pawprint.
  • Contribute positively to animal welfare through partnerships with shelters, rescue organizations, and conservation efforts.

By realizing this vision, we aim to create a better world for pets and the people who love them—one product at a time.

Our Custom Order Process

  1. Welcome to consult – Reach out with your needs—our team is here to listen and understand your vision.

  5. Arrange production – Our factory gets to work—producing your custom products with care, speed, and precision.
